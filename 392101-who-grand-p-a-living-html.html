Who is Grand P?

Grand P was born Moussa Sandiana Kaba. He is a famous singer, politician, and social media personality from Guinea, Africa.

How old is Grand P?

Grand P's age is 33 years as of July 2024. He was born on 11th October 1990, and his Zodiac sign is Libra.

Where is Grand P from?

The artist was born and raised in Sanguiana, Nabaya, Guinea. He never had the chance to go to school, so he decided to pursue his passions: music and acting.

Career history

Grand P became famous in 2019 during a show organized by one of his idols, Kerfalla Kante, at the People's Palace in Conakry. His popularity grew, enabling him to receive regular invitations to concerts and events.

What does Grand P do?

Guinean musician Grand P earns money from his music royalties and performing at events and concerts. As of July 2024, he has released only one album, I Khadi Na Na.

What condition does Grand P have?

The singer was born with a rare genetic disease called progeria. The condition is depicted by premature aging, which begins in childhood. About 350 to 400 people have it globally. People with this condition are more likely to suffer hip dislocation, arthritis, plaque buildup in the arteries, cataracts, and heart disease.

Grand P's illness affected his childhood and daily life since the disease is little known in his home country. He was marginalized, ridiculed, and rejected due to his different appearance.
