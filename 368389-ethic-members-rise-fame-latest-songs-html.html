Ethic: members, rise to fame, latest songs

June 2024 Â· 4 minute read

Ethic Entertainment is undeniably the biggest Kenyan music crew. Since they released their 2018 instant hit titled Lamba Lolo, the talented youths from Eastlands, Nairobi have experienced a meteoric rise. At the moment, Ethic music group enjoys a massive social media following, and their videos are among the most viewed in the country.

Despite Ethic songs facing controversy after controversy, this four-person crew has continued to spread the ethic Gengetone vibe through their lyricism and entertaining beats.

Who are Ethic members?

The crew has four members, all-male. Ethic real names are Rekless, Swat, Seska, and Zilla.

Rekless

He is Ethic Entertainment's main man. The talented star is known for his lyrical prowess, and usually handles the group's catchy choruses. A while ago, he was rumoured to have quit his crew.

The story got its credence after the star released a collabo with Shigha Ree-both and Kanari titled 'Kesi Baadae'. But these rumours were rubbished by the crew's official DJ, DJ Kasha. True to his word, Ethic Entertainment is still one of the most successful music groups.

Swat

Also going by the nickname "Mtoto wa Eunice", Swat is also another member of the group. The lyricist thrives in vulgarity, and his fans adore him for that. His birthday is on 18th April.

Swat has attracted his fair share of controversy. He was once subjected to mob justice, and he came out with serious injuries. The group manager later clarified that it was a case of mistaken identity.

Seska

The rapper made headlines after there was talk that he had succumbed. But the group refuted the claims, later on, claiming that the rumours were unfounded and their team member was alive. His birthday is on 10th July.

Zilla

The rapper's presence may not be as significant as that of other members of Ethic Entertainment. However, he is known for his lyrical prowess.

Even before forming the group, some of the Ethic members were friends already, having studied in the same schools.

The rapid growth of Ethic's music career

In its third year since inception, the four-member team has built a name for itself in the country's music industry as well as in the East African region. A section of Kenyan music fans has gone ahead to crown them the pioneers of the new wave Gengetone. But where did their journey start?

It all began in May 2018 when the 'Lamba Lolo' hitmakers dropped their first track. The tune went viral and grew to become an internet sensation. The title was even added to the 'Sheng dictionary.' Thanks to this major hit, the team was able to cut a niche for themselves in the competitive industry despite being the latest kids in town.

Ethic Entertainment songs list
