'Boko Haram Will Be Child's Play If You Do Anyhow'

March 2024 · 2 minute read

The Governor of Rivers State, Siminalayi Fubara has been warned by former militant leader, Asari Dokubo to stay clear of his business.

This is coming days after Dokubo was spotted in a video addressing a private army which he claimed works for President Bola Tinubu.

Dokubo in the latest viral video stated that Governor Fubara would face serious issues than Boko Haram if he does not stay clear of his business.

The ex-militant leader also claimed that the governorship election that brought Fubara in was rigged.

Dokubo, while reacting to reports that the Rivers State Governor has given the military a marching order to clampdown on his private army, said, “If yanga dey sleep, trouble go come wake am…em go collect..If you do anyhow, you see anyhow. Boko Haram will be a child’s play.

“Because we no look for somebody trouble. We dey on our own. We dey even help, e say make we no help. Him dey talk because them rig election put am somewhere.

“Wetin we do Sim? Them rig election, He is in government house. The person wey dey beat that drum with am, make him know the wood when they beat drum sometimes e dey break.

“We nor look for him trouble, make e nor look for our trouble o because if him look for our trouble, him go collect’. Under the constitution of Nigeria, we equal to am. Him nor be anybody. He is just an ordinary citizen just like Dr. Asari”
