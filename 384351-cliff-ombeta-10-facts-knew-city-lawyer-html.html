Cliff Ombeta: 10 facts you never knew about the city lawyer

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Cliff Ombeta is known far and wide when it comes to matters of law in Kenya. He is fondly called "Cliff,' and he is among the toughest criminal lawyers that come in handy at your time of need. He is known to fight against all odds to ensure his clients get due justice.

When you say that a person learns from experience, you need to have Cliff Ombeta in mind. He has come from far to be among the crème de la crème of lawyers in Kenya. His portfolio has many controversial cases that most lawyers shy away from. One of the cases is the famous Chris Obado case.

10 facts you never knew about the city lawyer

Cliff Ombeta is a lawyer who does not stand on the sidelines regarding representing his clients. Many people have wondered why he does that and would want to know what pushes him to go the extra mile. Here are 10 facts that you may not have known about him.

1. He is Kenyan

Lawyer Cliff Ombeta biography is brief. He was born in Kenya's western region and is a Kisii by tribe. He has kept his life private, and little is known about his family. He has one sister named Nyakerario Mayaka.

2. He went to several secondary schools

He started his high school at Jamhuri High School in Nairobi, where he was expelled when in form one. His expulsion was because he refused to be disciplined by his teacher. He later joined Highway High School in Nairobi, where he was expelled while in form 2 due to in-discipline issues.

Cliff later joined Friends School Kamusinga, where he got several suspensions. He left the school to join Koilel Secondary School, where he finished his high school education. He later joined Nairobi University, where he graduated with a bachelor's degree in Law.

3. He is divorced

Cliff Ombeta divorce is a question on many people's minds. Cliff Ombeta wife is not known at the moment. The reason for their divorce was due to irreconcilable differences. He has three children; one daughter and two boys, who they co-parent together with his ex-wife. Cliff insists he is not re-marrying.

4. He is a criminal lawyer

The book Rage of Angels authored by Sidney Sheldon, ignited the interest of Ombeta to become a lawyer. The book inspired him to be a criminal lawyer. His criminal law career started in 1995 after his graduation from The University of Nairobi. He has a stand of representing crimes that do not involve sexual assault and rape. Cliff Ombeta law firm is named Ombeta and Associates Law firm.

5. Cliff is rich

Ombeta is one of the wealthiest lawyers in Kenya. He is very private about his investments. Cliff Ombeta net worth is estimated at Kshs 300million. This estimate is derived from the number of cases he has won and represented and paid for.
