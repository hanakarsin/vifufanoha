Billionaire Aliko Dangote shows off dance moves in New York

July 2024 · 2 minute read

- Nigerian entertainer, Teni, was recently in New York to perform some of her heat singles

- Billionaire Aliko Dangote was also at the event and when Teni started singing, he burst a move

- The clip of him dancing has gone viral on social media

Most people agree that one cannot listen to Teni perform and still be on their seat. Just recently, the singer was in New York to do what she knows how to do best and billionaire Aliko Dangote was there to enjoy the party.

In a clip that is now making rounds on social media, the billionaire can be seen bursting a move as Teni rendered her hit single, titled, Case. Dangote, who looked dapper in a black suit had his attention on Teni as they both danced and enjoyed the simple things of life.

On many occasion, Teni has had the opportunity of meeting Dangote, and on each has shown her love for the multi-billionaire by singing for his entertainment and even adding his name in the lyrics of her hit single.
