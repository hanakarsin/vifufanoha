Mike Sonko: Kipchumba Murkomen, Mutula Kilonzo Junior among Nairobi governor's lawyers

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

- Mike Sonko was apprehended on Friday, December 6, after DPP Noordin Haji ordered for his arrest

- The governor was arraigned on Monday, December 6, where a team of 12 defence lawyers led by Senator Kipchumba Murkomen were set to represent him

- The governor was accused of numerous economic crimes among them money laundering, unlawful acquisition of public property among others

- Heavy security around Milimani law court where he was presented was witnessed on Monday, hours before his arraignment

Nairobi Governor Mike Sonko on the morning of Monday, December 9, appeared at the Milimani law courts to answer to graft related charges.

The flamboyant county boss armed himself with a team of 12 defence lawyers led by the Senate Majority Leader Kipchumba Murkomen, senator Mutula Kilonzo, Nelson Havi, MP Dan Maanzo MP among others.

Other lawyers included Cecil Miller, George Kithi, Alphonse Mutinda, Osundwa Michael, Harrison Kinyanjui and three others.

The governor also received overwhelming support from various politicians such as Embakasi East MP Babu Owino and Kericho Senator Aaron Cheruiyot who attended court.

According to a charge sheet, the governor was accused of more than 10 corruption and abuse of office offences.
