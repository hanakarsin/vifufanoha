Company services

eCitizen was launched in 2014. The platform was and still is a good concept of providing cross-agency, citizen-centric information and services, to help users complete your transactions conveniently with ONE government.

eCitizen is an initiative by the Ministry of Finance and is managed by the ICT Authority of Kenya(IDA). Currently, the eCitizen portal offers over 15 services that include:

  • Business Name Registration
  • Notice of Marriage
  • Interim Driving License
  • Solemnization of marriage
  • Provisional Driving License
  • Issuance of a registrar’s certificate
  • Driving Test Booking
  • Commissioning of affidavits
  • Business Name Search
  • Driving Class Endorsement
  • Special licenses for marriage
  • Land Rent Clearance Certificate
  • Driving License Information Corrections
  • Application for Passport and Application for Kenyan Visa
  • Driving License Renewal (1 Year)
  • Official Search (Nairobi Blocks)
  • Duplicate Driving License
