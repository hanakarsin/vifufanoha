&quot;Wizkid Done Dey Lecture You?&quot; Reactions As Davido Reveals His Motto For 2022

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Multiple award-winning Nigerian musician and philanthropist, Davido has revealed what will be his watchword for the year 2022.

Davido who made the revelation on Tuesday in a post on his Twitter account said he wants to make money and ignore this year.

He wrote: “This year’s motto: Make money and ignore.”

See the original post below.

This years motto : Make money and ignore

— Davido (@davido) January 11, 2022

The post as expected has attracted lots of reactions from his fans and followers including others on the micro-blogging app with some suggesting Davido wants to start doing what Wizkid has been doing for over five years.
