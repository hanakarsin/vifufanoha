Joey Graziadei's Yellow Eyes Have Some Fans Thinking He's in Liver Failure, but He's OK

While many have swooned over his sensitivity, a few 'Bachelor' fans are worried about Joey's liver.

By Joseph Allen

Updated March 1 2024, 4:18 p.m. ET

Although the season is not over yet, Joey Graziadei has already gotten plenty of rave reviews on The Bachelor. Thanks in part to what appear to be genuine sensitivity and emotional intelligence, Joey has handled his interactions with the show's women with incredible grace, even though the process that it puts them through can be highly emotional.

Even as Joey has gotten generally positive reviews from fans, there are also many who are wondering about some details of the show that have relatively little to do with who Joey might ultimately end up with. One such topic has to do with Joey's eyes, and why they seem to be so yellow.

Why does Joey Graziadei have yellow eyes?

If you watch Joey carefully on the show, or even just look at pictures of him, you may notice that he appears to have a pretty distinct yellow color in his irises. Fans have taken note of this incredibly unusual color, and many have speculated about what could be causing it, including a very popular theory that Joey is jaundiced, which often causes yellowing of the eyes and skin.

In a recent interview with Us Weekly, Joey addressed the speculation around his eyes without offering much detail on what exactly was causing it.

Thankfully, that video came on Feb. 29, 2024. In a clip posted to Joey's Instagram, he explains that the story behind his yellow eyes stems back to high school. During his adolescent years, Joey says he became sick for over a week. Naturally, his mom took him to get checked out by his doctor.

After having his blood drawn, it was revealed that Joey's bilirubin count was very high, so doctors had him undergo a liver ultrasound. While they were able to determine that nothing was seriously wrong, Joey says they did diagnose him with Gilbert syndrome, which affects the white part of his eyes. However, he says that he is "healthy."

The lead for Season 28 of The Bachelor also mentioned that the condition is "worse when I have stress or a lack of sleep" and is something he wants to pay more attention to going forward.

Is Joey the best Bachelor ever?

Thanks to his emotional sensitivity, and the fact that many fans of the show find him genuinely dreamy, many are wondering whether Joey may in fact be the best Bachelor in the show's history.

Of course, there's still plenty of time for things to take a wrong turn before the season is over.

In the meantime, though, fans are delighting in the fact that a man is capable of being both attractive and relatively attuned to what the people around him are telling him about what they need. It's an unusual combination, even on a show that's meant to be as romantic as The Bachelor, but one that many are loving.

Now that fans know why the white's of Joey's eyes appear yellow at times, they can sit back and wait to see how Season 28 wraps up.
