Twins drown

Barely one month ago, two Kwale twin brothers died in the Indian Ocean.

The 18-year-olds Kennedy Gichuhi and Solomon Macharia had gone swimming at Gazi Beach on Monday, May 31, when they were swept by strong waves.

The divers who retrieved the remains of the duo divulged that the boys’ arms were locked in each other in what appeared like a tight embrace.

Their father, Samuel Gatindu confirmed the tragic incident and revealed his sons had left the family farm whey they worked in the afternoon to go and unwind at the beach.

"We were with them at the farm about three o'clock. I was later informed they had drowned in the ocean," Samuel sadly said.
