Company services

AMACO is a general insurance firm which was formed to offer different types of insurance services to merchants. In simple terms, the company sought to offer a wide range of insurance solutions for companies that are interested in the Kenyan economy. Since its inception, the company has developed and become a leading insurer in Kenya. One of the key elements that make them stand out is their wide product portfolio; this makes them one of the most creative insurance companies. Other than this, AMACO boasts of a long list of success in the current Kenyan market, and they are proud of the place they hold in the insurance sector.

African Merchant Assurance Company provides products such as Travelling Insurance, Merchant Commercial, Burglary Insurance, Consequential Loss, Contractors All Risks, Public Liability, and Professional Indemnity, among other services. The company continues to innovate and produce more products to suit the ever-growing Kenyan insurance market. This company is recommended mainly for business people due to their long history and their specialty products designed for merchants. Nevertheless, they are a credible insurance provider of general coverage in the Kenyan market.
