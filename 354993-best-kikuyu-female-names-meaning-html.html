The Kikuyu naming system for females

The Kikuyu naming system is unique from other local tribes because it emphasizes on personal attributes. For this reason, Kikuyu female names naturally match the characteristics of most women bearing them. The Kikuyu girl child naming is highly consistent with rare exceptions. Favourite Kikuyu female names and their meaning

What do Kikuyu female names mean? The daughters of Mumbi are widespread and outspoken on local media and other platforms for their industrious nature. Some of their names, such as Wanjiku, have earned national sympathy to represent an ordinary Kenyan when making crucial political and economic references. Even with the popularity of Kikuyu female names, not many people know much about their underlying meaning.

1. Mumbi

Mumbi means the potter or the one who moulds. This is one of the best Kikuyu names because it refers to the mother of the Kikuyu nation.

2. Njambi

She is the queen of intelligence. Her hard-working nature and strong spirit make her a good wife. She loves solitude and never wears out.

3. Njoki

Njoki means the one who resurrected. These women are best known for their hard work and infectiously social nature. Njoki's male equivalent names are Kariuki or Muchoki.

4. Nyambura

Nyambura means of rain. These ladies are loyal and loving to those around them. They generally make good wives and smile less often, at least genuinely.

5. Muthoni

The meaning of Muthoni in Kikuyu is an in-law. These ladies are exceptionally brilliant and prefer to remain in charge of their own destiny. Their enlightened and secretive social nature is conspicuous in their lifestyle. They are highly affectionate and have a strong tendency to love sincerely and deeply. However shy, ladies called Muthoni make good leaders and are highly receptive to the needs of others.

6. Njeri

The name means travelling or one who is always on the move, simply termed as the name that means wanderer. Ladies called Njeri portray strong emotions and fast reactions with a strong sense of adventure and involvement in different activities. Njeri is beautiful for her alluring figures and noticeable features.

7. Wambui

Translated to zebra or of stripes, the meaning of Wambui in Kikuyu is the singer of songs. These lasses always love pleasure, over-indulge, and are impulsive in their character. Their social nature and knowledgeable skills are their strengths. They hardly smile but are secretly good at it. They are generally flexible with sharp memories that make them good friends or partners.

8. Wangui

Wangui, originally one of Mumbi's nine daughters, means the baby carrier. She is a true image of the peacock. Ladies called Wangui have excellent conversation skills and are hopeful. Their arguably restless nature makes them good at what they do best. They lead a balanced lifestyle with a good sense of emotional maturity.

9. Wangari

The name means of the leopard, fierce, or tough. These ladies are often calm in their affair and how they crack their laughter. They can become pressing at times but are mostly charming and lovable. They rank highly as wife materials.

10. Wangeci

The name Wangeci is associated with pioneering. She is ambitious, confident, loves challenges, and never fails at what she does. Their ambitious and determined nature makes them good pioneers and leaders. They show a strong commitment to friendships and relationships.

11. Wanjiku

What is the meaning of Wanjiku in Kikuyu? The meaning of Wanjiku is unclear, but a common characteristic of these women is that they are orderly and hard working.
