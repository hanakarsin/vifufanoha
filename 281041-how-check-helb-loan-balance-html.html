How to check your HELB loan balance: a detailed guide

February 2024 · 5 minute read

The Higher Education Loans Board (HELB) is a statutory body that grants loans, bursaries, and scholarships for training in universities and TVET institutions in Kenya. As a former student or a current student who's taken up a loan, you might be caught in a situation where you need to check your HELB loan balance. If you want to know how to check your HELB loan balance, make sure to read this article.

How to check your HELB loan balance via SMS

The Higher Education Loans Board has come up with a way of checking your loan balance via SMS. This option is convenient for people who do not have access to the Internet. The process is detailed below.

  • Register for the service by sending the words REGISTER#ID No” to 5122. Ensure you put the hash (#) sign between the word REGISTER and your ID Number.
  • After registration, compose a new word in your SMS.
  • Type the hash sign (#).
  • Type your ID number.
  • Send the SMS to the number 5122.

NB: This method works for those who register their details on HELB’s online database using the exact phone number they will be using to check their loan balance.

How to check your HELB loan balance via USSD

Here is how to check your HELB loan balance via USSD.

  • Dial *642# on your phone.
  • Select entry 1 and send.
  • Enter the details required in the next interface. These include your first and last names, your ID number, and your date of birth.
  • Enter your PIN and confirm it.
  • You will receive a success notification saying, “Thank you for registering with the HELB mobile platform. You will receive SMS confirmation shortly.”
  • Once registered, dial the USSD code again, i.e., *642#.
  • Select entry 2 for Loan Repayment.
  • Select Loan Balance to know your loan status.

How to check your HELB loan balance online

If you have been wondering how to confirm your HELB loan balance, you can do it online using the steps documented below.

  • Visit the HELB portal on an internet-enabled device.
  • Enter your details to log into your account.
  • Click on Loan Reports.
  • Click on Statement. You can view your balance.
  • If you wish to print it, click Print to generate a PDF copy.

NB: You can also use the HELB portal to check your loan disbursement status.

How to check your HELB balance using the app

Use the steps below to confirm your balance using the HELB app, also known as the HELB status checker.

  • Download the HELB app on your smartphone.
  • Open the app and follow the prompts to complete the registration process.
  • Once registered, enter your details to log into your account.
  • Click on Loan Reports.
  • Click on Statement to view your balance.

How do you check if your loan is approved?

To check if you have been allocated the funding, you should log in to the HELB portal through the Helb App, USSD or the web. HELB sends an SMS alert when funds are disbursed to the account.

How does HELB work?

HELB works by offering loans to students pursuing professional courses, diploma, certificate, artisan, higher national diploma, bachelors, and postgraduate degrees in Kenya. The loans are awarded per year and paid directly to various institutions of higher learning.

Can you repay your HELB loan in a single instalment?

Yes, you can repay your loan in one single instalment instead of monthly instalments. HELB will not charge extra fees for early repayment.

What happens once you fully repay your loan?

HELB will issue a clearance certificate if you have repaid the loan in full. All that you need to do is visit the HELB portal and submit your request for clearance.

Is HELB interest charged for Muslims?

Yes, HELB does not waive interest for Muslims because it does not apply the Shariah law. To receive a loan, you must agree to all terms and conditions.
