Here's Where You'll Find the Best National Cheeseburger Day Deals Near You

August 2024 · 3 minute read

These freebies and deals are the best way to celebrate National Cheeseburger Day 2019. Click to find out where the best deals are near you.

The greatest day of the year is about to be upon us because Wednesday, Sept. 18 is National Cheeseburger Day and there are all kinds of deals and freebies that are sure to make it the absolute best holiday. 

Keep scrolling for your comprehensive guide of all the best spots to hit up to enjoy as many hamburgers as your stomach can handle. We recommend arriving early and very hungry.

National Cheeseburger Day 2019 freebies and deals:

Applebee's

Applebee's is offering endless fries when you order a classic burger or classic cheeseburger for $6.99 on Wednesday.

B. Good

Download the B. Good app, and get a free BBQ Bacon, Power Play, or Mushroom & Swiss burger all the way through Sept. 22.

Burger 21

Burger 21 is bringing back its limited-edition Super Cheesy burger to participating locations for one day only on Sept. 21.

BurgerFi

Visit your local BurgerFi and buy one cheeseburger to get a second cheeseburger for just $1. Scroll their website and mention or show the offer to redeem it. It's not available online, in-app, or on third-party orders.

Burger Libre

"Come join us this Wednesday for #NationalCheeseburgeryDay with $3 Classicos all day," writes Burger Libre's Twitter account. "Yep, that's $3 CLASSICOS all DAY Wednesday."

Burger King

Download the Burger King app and receive an offer for a 59¢ cheeseburger when you order on mobile. While the fine print says it's one burger per customer, Hip2Save claims they were able to order multiple cheeseburgers in just one visit. 

Dairy Queen

DQ is offering its new two for $4 Super Snack deal on National Cheeseburger Day, which includes your choice of mix and match snacks. We recommend getting the cheeseburger, of course.

DogHaus

Between 11 a.m. and 10 p.m. on Sept. 18, get a free double patty with cheese when you scan your Dog Haus app at the nearest location to you.

Farmer Boys

Say it's Cheeseburger Day and you'll get a $2 cheeseburger on Wednesday starting at 2 p.m.

Habit Burger Grill

Habit is using Cheeseburger Day for charity. When you make a donation of $2 or more to No Kid Hungry, you'll get a coupon for a free Charburger with cheese you can use on your next visit.

Houlihan's Restaurant and Bar

Houlihan's is offering a $5 cheeseburger and fries all day to celebrate the holiday.

Johnny Rockets

Sign up for the Johnny Rockets E-Club and you'll get an email for a completely free burger with the purchase of an entree and drink, or a shake!

Jack in the Box

Download the app and you'll find a coupon good for a free burger of your choice to use on Wednesday only.

Miller's Ale House

Dine in at Miller's and enjoy a classic cheeseburger with seasoned fries for $5.99. This offer will be really short-lived though, from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. on Wednesday.

Red Robin

Red Robin is getting in on the National Cheeseburger Day fun with an offer you'll want to take a bite out of. Buy a beverage and enjoy a gourmet cheeseburger and bottomless steak fries for only $5.

Wendy's

Wendy's uses its Twitter account for more than just brutally roasting every other fast food brand in existence. This year, they announced they'd be offering guests a BOGO deal that applies to Dave's single and Dave's doubles for any takeout and dine-in customers.

Enjoy! We can't wait to make the rounds.
