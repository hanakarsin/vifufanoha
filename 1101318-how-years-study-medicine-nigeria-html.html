Any student who effectively finished his or her studies in Medicine and Surgery will, in the long run, become a Medical Doctor with the degree.

Skill Level Undergraduate Degree

The course span is six years for UTME Students and five years for Direct Entry Students beginning from level 200 (year two).

Subjects needed to study medicine

Medicine and Surgery is one the most competitive course to get access to in Nigeria. However, candidates who made the core admission requirements are offered admission into 6 or 5-year program depending on Mode of entry (UTME or DE).

Although, admission requirements slightly differ in many schools, here are the basic entry as well as waiver considerations.

UTME Requirements to Study Medicine And Surgery In Nigeria

Candidates who wish to gain admission into Medicine and Surgery program at any University in Nigeria must have at least Five (5) SSCE (WAEC/NECO) credit passes in English Language, Mathematics, Physics Chemistry, and Biology. Some schools accept a combination of O Level results.

JAMB-UTME Subject Combination to Study Medicine and Surgery in Nigeria.

The JAMB UTME Subject combination for Medicine and Surgery are Use of English, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.

Special Admission Considerations for JAMB UTME Candidates

  • UNN accepts Physics with Chemistry (as a single subject) for Physics and Chemistry. UNN requires that you passs in one sitting.
  • UNILORIN requires five O Level credit (at only one sitting) and a minimum score of 240 in JAMB examination. .
  • BENIN, IBADAN, IMSU, ABSU, EBSU, UNIOSUN, ANSU, DELSU and BOWEN require the O Level Credits at one sitting.

    • UTME SUBJECTS: UDU and MAIDUGURI accept Mathematics instead of Physics.

    Direct Entry Requirements

    Candidates who wish to gain admission into Medicine and Surgery program at any University in Nigeria at direct entry level must have:

  • ‘A’ level passes in Physics, Chemistry and Biology or Zoology in addition to UTME requirements stated above.
  • Bachelor of Science Degree (BSC or BOS) in relevant fields.
