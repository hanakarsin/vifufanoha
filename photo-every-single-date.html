A Guy Took Pictures Of His Girlfriend On Every Date For A Year And Turned It Into the Perfect Gift

By Aimee Lutkin

Updated June 7 2021, 10:19 p.m. ET

It's hard to keep believing in romance in today's world, when we can dismiss half the dateable population with a swipe on our phones. Do people even really date for real? Do they stick it out? Do they do nice things for each other? It turns out they do, you just have to dig deep to find out about it.

We all aren't lucky enough to have a perfect relationship like Chrissy Teigen and John Legend, which combines love, food, and just the right amount of Twitter trolling. Seriously, remember that time he bought her a gigantic cheese wheel for Christmas or when Chrissy surprised John in bed with a cake for Father's Day? 

Well, Twitter user @magsster shared her boyrfriend's incredibly romantic gesture on their anniversary. It's unclear if they've only been together a year and he had a remarkable amount of foresight, or if he decided this was the year for a secret photo book, but its still unbelievably sweet.
