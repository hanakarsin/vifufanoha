  • A young Nigerian lady who read mineral and petroleum resources at Auchi Polytechnic has revealed why she became a taxi driver

A 22-year-old lady, Lucia Osamo, has revealed that she became a taxi driver because she could not afford KSh 1,300 to register for university intake.

In an exclusive interview with NigeriaLegit TV, she said that she sometimes gets hit on by some of her male customers who would say that she is too pretty to be doing her kind of work.

My experiences as a driver

Narrating one of her experiences with a female customer, she said when she showed up, the lady asked where the original driver is.
