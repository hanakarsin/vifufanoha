Former Machachari star Baha whose real name is Tyler Mbaya, is in a happy relationship with his girlfriend, Georgina Njenga. The two are madly in love with each other.

Speaking for the first time about how they met on Georgina's YouTube channel, the lovebirds gave an intriguing story.

Georgina, 21, said she was a big fan of Baha when he featured on Machachari TV series.

In 2016, while still in High School, Georgina noticed her cousin Nina and Baha were so close. After completing Form Four, Georgina said her cousin told her Baha was looking for a girlfriend and asked her to send her pics so she could show him.

She sent the photos, and Nina showed them to Baha.

"My cousin invited us to her place, and that's how we first met," she said.

On the other hand, Baha said he really wanted to settle down, but his two past relationships were just a fling before he met Georgina.

"Hii ready to mingle haikuwa inanipeleka mahali. Msee utahave fun but utacome kuona at what cost...(The relationships weren't serious. I told myself having just fun in life isn't enough)," Baha said.

He said he learnt from his late mum that respect in a relationship is based on how far you've come.

"Sikuona naona naeza pata dem mtrue na trustworthy mwenye naweza make life naye from grass to grace. I never thought I would get a good woman who I would build an empire with)," he narrated.

The multitalented actor said he asked Nina, who's married to one of his friends, to hook him up with someone ready to settle down because he didn't trust his other female friends.

Baha said he had been through a lot after COVID-19 ravaged the globe, and he told Nina to inform Georgina that he was not doing well financially before they met. He said:

"Nilimwambia mimi sina kakitu asicome na hizo expectations (I told her I was broke so Georgina shouldn't expect heaven on earth from him).

After meeting, they started hanging out, and all of a sudden, he wanted friends with benefits type of relationship, yet Georgina wasn't in for that.

"I was looking for a serious relationship," she said, adding, "at that time, there was a guy who was hitting on me, and I started dating him and stopped talking to Baha."

Georgina said Baha used to call her, asking for a meetup even though he knew she was in a relationship.

Georgina and her ex broke up after a few months of dating.

One Sunday, Georgina visited her friends in Roysambu, and she was caught up in the curfew hours. She was confused about how she was going to get home.

"Baha called me and asked me where I was and when I told him where I was, he came for me, and that's how we started dating," she said.

Baha showered his partner with love, describing her as his best friend, a keeper, kindhearted and a rare soul. He said he appreciates and treasures every moment spent together.
